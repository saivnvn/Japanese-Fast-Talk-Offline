## 🚀 Features

### ▶ Speak with 600+ Real Phrases
- 600+ everyday Japanese phrases ready to use  
- 20 real-life topics: daily, shopping, restaurant, travel, emergency…  
- Learn what people actually say — not textbook Japanese  

### ▶ Learn Real-Life Conversations
- Short, clear, and easy-to-use phrases  
- Perfect for beginners and travelers  
- Speak naturally from day one  

### ▶ Speak & Listen Offline
- Built-in Japanese pronunciation on your device  
- Slow mode for easier listening  
- No internet needed — learn anytime, anywhere  

### ▶ Learn Faster with Practice
- Flashcards: swipe, flip, shuffle  
- Quiz modes: multiple choice, matching, fill in the blank  
- Review mistakes to improve retention  

### ▶ Track Your Progress
- Daily learning streak  
- Progress by category  
- Accuracy and best streak  

### ▶ Learn Your Way
- Save favorite phrases  
- Mark phrases as learned  
- Search by Japanese, romaji, or English  

### ▶ Build a Daily Habit
- Daily phrases to keep learning  
- Reminder notifications  
- Home screen widgets for quick access
